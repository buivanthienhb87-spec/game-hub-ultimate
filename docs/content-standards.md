# Content Standards

All pages should be factual, readable, and useful to players or maintainers. Content must avoid plagiarism, unsafe automation, cheats, piracy, gambling, and unsupported reward claims.

| Standard | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Accuracy | Prefer official references and clearly mark opinion-based content. |
| Clarity | Use descriptive headings and short paragraphs. |
| Safety | Do not include harmful scripts or abuse instructions. |
| Maintenance | Include a review date and update stale pages when needed. |
